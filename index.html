About the Game

KATIPUDROID is a 3D role-playing game set during the Spanish colonization of the Philippines. You will take on missions inspired by the real-life struggles of the Katipuneros, blending history, action, and exploration into an immersive experience.

Characters

Macario Sakay

Macario Sakay was a member of the Katipunan who upheld its ideals of Philippine independence even after Spain's defeat.

Andres Bonifacio

Andrés Bonifacio was the founder and leader of the Katipunan, a secret revolutionary society that fought for Philippine independence from Spanish colonial rule.

Tandang Sora

Tandang Sora, whose real name was Melchora Aquino, was known as the Mother of the Philippine Revolution for aiding members of the Katipunan. She provided food, shelter, and medical care to revolutionaries, earning deep respect despite being exiled by Spanish authorities for her support.

Emilio Aguinaldo

Emilio Aguinaldo was the first president of the Philippines and a key leader in the struggle for independence from both Spain and the United States.
